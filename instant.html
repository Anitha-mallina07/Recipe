Instant Meals

  • Noodles

    • Ingredients

    1. 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
    2. 1/4 cup warm water (about 110°F or 45°C)
    3. 1 cup warm milk (about 110°F or 45°C)
    4. 1/4 cup granulated sugar
    5. 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
    6. 1/2 teaspoon salt
    7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    8. 2 large eggs
    9. 4 cups all-purpose flour (plus extra for dusting)
    10. Oil for frying (vegetable or canola oil)
    11. Powdered sugar or glaze for topping (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Activate the Yeast:
      • In a small bowl, combine the warm water and active dry yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until it becomes frothy.
    2. Prepare the Dough:

      • In a large mixing bowl, combine the warm milk, granulated sugar, melted butter, salt, vanilla extract, and eggs. Mix well. Add the frothy yeast mixture to the bowl and mix until combined. Gradually add the all-purpose flour, one cup at a time, mixing until a soft dough forms.

    3. Knead the Dough:
      • Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook attachment for this step.
    4. Let the Dough Rise:
      • Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
    5. Shape the Doughnuts:
      • Once the dough has risen, turn it out onto a floured surface and roll it to about 1/2-inch thickness. Use a doughnut cutter or two round cookie cutters (one large and one small) to cut out doughnut shapes. Gather the scraps, re-roll, and cut out more doughnuts.
    6. Second Rise:
      • Place the cut-out doughnuts and doughnut holes on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Cover with a kitchen towel and let them rise for another 30-45 minutes, or until they have puffed up.
    7. Fry the Doughnuts:
      • Heat oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C). Carefully add a few doughnuts at a time to the hot oil and fry for about 1-2 minutes on each side, or until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
    8. Glaze or Powder (Optional):
      • If desired, you can dip the warm doughnuts in powdered sugar or your favorite glaze.
